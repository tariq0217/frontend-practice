Marina Beach is the longest natural urban beach in the country and second longest globally, this 13 km stretch is the beating heart of Chennai. It is famous for its lively evening fairground atmosphere, street food (like sundal and fried fish), and historical monuments (including statues, the Lighthouse, and the Triumph of Labour).
