French Fries Receipt

Ingredients

  • Potatoes
  • Salt
  • Olive Oil

Instructions

  1. Peel the potatoes and cut them into thin strips.
  2. Rinse the potato strips in cold water to remove excess starch.
  3. Pat the potato strips dry with a clean towel.
  4. Heat olive oil in a deep fryer or large pot to 350°F (175°C).
  5. Fry the potato strips in batches until golden brown and crispy, about 3-4 minutes per batch.
  6. Remove the fries from the oil and place them on a paper towel-lined plate to drain excess oil.
  7. Season the fries with salt while they are still hot.
  8. Serve immediately and enjoy your homemade French fries!
Prep Time:
15 minutes
Cook Time:
10 minutes
Total Time:
25 minutes
